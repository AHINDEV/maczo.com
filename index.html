About Maczo Digital Store

Redefining Tech, One Refurbished Device at a Time

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Maczo began as a passion project between three tech enthusiasts who believed in making premium Apple technology accessible to everyone. What started in a small garage in San Francisco has now transformed into a trusted name in refurbished electronics across India.

We combine cutting-edge technology with sustainable practices to deliver devices that perform like new while reducing electronic waste. Every product we sell is a step toward a greener planet.
